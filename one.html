Our Next Energy (ONE) is a company focused on revolutionizing energy storage solutions for electric vehicles (EVs) and the grid. They develop advanced battery technologies like the Gemini™ dual-chemistry battery, which significantly extends EV range, and the Aries™ series, which offers sustainable, durable, and efficient energy storage options. Their mission centers on enhancing range, safety, and sustainability, utilizing abundant materials to build a robust U.S. supply chain for renewable energy.

I played a key role in shaping the website’s structure, developing front-end pages via DatoCMS, and coordinating content from photographers, videographers, and graphic designers. Additionally, I designed email marketing templates, crafted internal and external presentation decks, and took ownership of the in-house signage design system. My contributions ensured cohesive branding across all digital and physical touchpoints, enhancing both the user experience and internal marketing efforts.
